About the North Pole

The North Pole is a place where you can meet the magnificent natural environment and unique ecosystems that are nowhere to be found. Most of us may never have a chance to visit the North Pole.

Even in this crisis, some companies are trying to develop the North Pole for oil drilling. But if our voices for the protection of the North Pole get louder, we can prevent the worst. The most important time to protect the North Pole and the Earth is now.

However, even so far away, the Arctic ecosystem is affecting the lives of all of us, the entire planet.Due to global warming, Arctic temperatures are rising rapidly, and more glaciers are melting every year.

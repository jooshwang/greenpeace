WE ARE GREENPEACE

Greenpeace has a mission for a green and peaceful world. Find out more about our organisation with the links below.

  • VALUES & MISSION Greenpeace is an independent, nonprofit, global campaigning organization that uses non-violent, creative confrontation to expose global environmental problems and their causes.
  • HISTORY Greenpeace has been in East Asia since 1997 to fight climate change, stop toxic pollution, ensure food security, end illegal deforestation and defend the oceans.
  • ACHIEVEMENTS Greenpeace in East Asia took a series of actions to fight climate change, stop toxic pollution, ensure food security, end illegal deforestation, and defend the oceans.
  • ANNUAL REPORT Greenpeace East Asia is proud to be part of a global network of independent Greenpeace organisations and we are happy to report back on our work.

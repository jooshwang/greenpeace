Cause and effect

Climate change is an extremely important issue in East Asia, where the world's largest greenhouse gas is generated.Climate change is the reality that we are facing right now. Climate change is occurring as a human activity.

Especially, burning fossil fuels such as coal and oil is a problem. We are witnessing the effects of climate change, including severe cold and hot weather, rising sea levels, and the disappearance of Antarctic and Arctic glaciers. But if we act together, we will be able to stop the recovery of climate change. Many people living in East Asia are threatened by global warming and climate change, from melted glaciers to droughts and floods.
